Our Recruitment Process

At HireVanta Recruitment Services, we follow a structured and transparent hiring process to ensure quality placements and long-term value for our clients.

  • Requirement Understanding: Detailed discussion to understand role expectations, skills, budget, and timelines.
  • Candidate Sourcing: Talent sourcing through internal database, job portals, referrals, and targeted searches.
  • Screening & Shortlisting: Resume evaluation, telephonic screening, and candidate validation.
  • Interview Coordination: Smooth scheduling and feedback coordination between client and candidate.
  • Offer & Closure: Offer negotiation support, joining follow-ups, and documentation.
  • Post-Joining Support: Replacement support as per agreed terms.
